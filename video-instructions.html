Video Preparation Guidelines

Follow these essential guidelines to ensure your video presentation meets our technical requirements and maintains the highest professional standards.

  • File Format: Submit your video as a single MP4 file for optimal compatibility
  • File Size Limit: Maximum file size of 500MB to ensure smooth upload and playback
  • Patient Privacy: Ensure all patient information is anonymized and HIPAA compliant
  • Audio Requirements: Include clear audio narration - voice-over is encouraged for better presentation
  • Video Quality: Minimum 720p resolution recommended for clear viewing
  • Duration: Keep presentations between 10-15 minutes for optimal engagement

Submission Process

Follow these step-by-step instructions to submit your video presentation:

  1. Navigate to our secure upload portal at https://www.wetransfer.com/
  2. Name your file using this format: "COS2025-[Your-submission-code].mp4"
  3. Use support@scigentech.com as the recipient email address
  4. Enter the same email address you used for your abstract submission
  5. Include this message text:
    "Video Submission for The Clinical Orthopaedic Society 113th Annual Meeting - [Your submission code]"
  6. Review all information carefully, then click "Transfer" to submit your file
